HOW TO PLAY

Controls: Use the left joystick to drive your boat. Drag anywhere on the right side of the screen to look around.

Shooting: Hold the red button to aim your cannon. Release the button to fire at the red target.

Upgrades: You get +5 Coins for every piece you blast off an enemy ship, and +10 for a direct hit to the cannon! Spend them in the shop to upgrade stats.

Radar: Once purchased, a minimap appears. Blue = Allies, Red = Enemies. Tap the minimap to expand it fullscreen.

PRO TIP: If you hit an enemy boat's CANNON directly, their entire ship will instantly explode!
