HOW TO PLAY

🕹️ Controls: Use the left joystick to drive your boat. Drag anywhere on the right side of the screen to look around.

🎯 Shooting: Hold the red button to aim your cannon. Release the button to fire at the red target circle.

🪙 Upgrades: You get +5 Coins for every piece you blast off an enemy ship. Spend them in the shop to upgrade speed and reload times!

💥 PRO TIP: If you manage to hit an enemy boat's CANNON directly, their entire ship will instantly explode and be eliminated!

🏆 Winning: Be the last boat standing, or have the most pieces destroyed when the 2:00 timer runs out.
